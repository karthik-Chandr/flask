Overview

AWSome Builder Scheduler is a tool that will facilitate the process of selecting or searching for a number of panelists for the AWSome Builder presentation. When there is a need for selecting a number of consultants, AWSome Builder Scheduler helps to schedule a panel for the AWSome Builder presentation and automatically provides the potential panelist's list based on the ProServe Consultant’s availability and skills. This tool will send an email to the proposed panelists with a link to confirm their availability for the AWSome Builder panel.

User Guide

1. How to create a panel

Step 1: Go to Home page and add the tentative meeting dates, required panelist skills, and the number of panelists required and submit the form.

Step 2: Once the required details are submitted, the panel initiator can send an email to each individual separately by clicking ‘Send E-mail’, to confirm their presence in the following AWSome Builder meeting.


2. How to replace a declined panelist

Step 1: Go to ‘Scheduled Meetings’, find the panelist to be replaced and click on ‘Modify’.


Step 2: Click on ‘Replace’ to give the confirmation.


Step 3: Verify the new panelist details and click on ‘Send E-mail’ to complete the process to replace the declined panelist.
